The Problem We Solve

Smallholder farmers in Central Kenya face high feed costs due to retail pricing and individual transport expenses. By pooling orders within zones, Santamore Feeds enables farmers to access wholesale rates and shared delivery—reducing costs by up to 15% while building stronger farming communities.
